Domain Umbrella Routing

TalentGate manages central email routing for client Labor Market Testing (LMT). To receive applicant emails natively (e.g. at vltran@careers.talentgate.com.au), configure the MX and TXT records on the company's DNS server below. Our SMTP parser processes incoming messages and pushes them here instantly via webhooks.

Record Type Host/Name Target Value Priority Status
MX @ mx.talentgate.com.au 10 Verified
TXT (SPF) @ v=spf1 include:mail.talentgate.com.au ~all - Verified
TXT (DKIM) tg._domainkey k=rsa; p=MIGfMA0GCSqGSIb3DQEBAQUAA4GN... - Verified
TXT (DMARC) _dmarc v=DMARC1; p=quarantine; pct=100 - Verified

Mail Webhook Parser Endpoint

For advanced developers, bypass SMTP setup and post email payloads (MIME parsed) directly to this REST API webhook route:

POST https://talentgate.com.au/.netlify/functions/inbound?mailbox=vltran@careers.talentgate.com.au
