About TalentGate

TalentGate is Australia’s modern bridge between employers and skilled workers. Powered by Dojo Legal, we combine legal, migration, and recruitment expertise for a seamless experience.

Our team includes lawyers, recruiters, compliance experts, and settlement advisors—all working together to deliver real results.

To lift clients above legal and migration challenges, empowering them with empathy, expertise, and unwavering integrity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you charge recruitment or placement fees?

A: No, our model is based on transparent, fixed legal and strategy fees only.

Q: What industries do you serve?

A: Agriculture, food processing, healthcare, construction, hospitality, logistics, professional services, and more.

Q: Do you guarantee job or PR outcomes?

A: We never make false promises. We only proceed with ethical, realistic pathways.

Q: Can you help with settlement and relocation?

A: Yes, our settlement concierge covers banking, housing, schooling, and local integration.

Q: How long does the process take?

A: Timelines vary by service. Contact us for a tailored assessment.

Q: What happens after I make contact?

A: We triage your needs, provide honest advice, and guide you step-by-step.
